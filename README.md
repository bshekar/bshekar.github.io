## Bharath Shekar

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Earth Sciences, IIT Bombay since February, 2014. I completed my PhD in Geophysics from the [Center for Wave Phenomena](https://cwp.mines.edu/), Colorado School of Mines in November, 2013. I graduated with an Integrated M.Sc in Exploration Geophysics from [IIT Kharagpur](http://www.iitkgp.ac.in/) in 2008. I primarily work on inverse problems connected to seismic wave propagation.
